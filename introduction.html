Buried Secrets

Charles Kingston mysteriously lost his life 4 years ago after what was supposed to be a joyous celebration of success, however a build up of extreme tension finally boiled over on that one night. The case was quickly ruled as an unfortunate suicide, however your team have suspicions.

Cause of death: **Suspected Overdose**

Three suspects were present in the house:

  • Margaret Kingston - Charles' wife
  • Victor Langston - A former doctor who lost his license
  • Jonathan Donaghy - Ex Business partner on volatile terms

You have been hired as a private investigator to finally finish this case.You must submit your evidence you find using the submit button in your notebook to put the real killer behind bars.
