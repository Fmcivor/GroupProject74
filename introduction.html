Buried Secrets

Charles Kingston was mysteriously murdered 4 years ago after what was supposed to be a joyous celebration of success, however a build up of extreme tension finally boiled over on that one night. The case was quickly ruled as an unfortunate suicide.

Cause of death: **Suspected Overdose**

Three suspects were present in the house:

  • Margaret Kingston - The estranged wife
  • Dr. Victor Langston - A family doctor with secrets
  • Jonathan Donaghy - Ex Business partner on volatile terms

You have been hired as a private investigator to finally finish this case. You have 10 minutes to search the mansion and find sufficient evidence to accuse the murderer.
